## Reuse Conclusions

The rebuild should create new enterprise orchestration, workflow, gate, RBAC, tenancy, and traceability services, but it should not rebuild standard design, generation-station design, BOQ, marketplace, funding, finance, reporting, digital twin, tutorials, audit, AI gateway, or deployment plumbing. The largest risks are tenancy conversion, background processing, and enforcing doc-3 controls at service level rather than UI level.
