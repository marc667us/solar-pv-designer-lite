# Loom Shell #3 — Send to Client in 30 seconds

**Audience:** electrical contractor / solar installer who has just finished a cost estimate or PV design.
**Goal:** prove they can email the deliverable to the client (with Excel + PDF attached) in under thirty seconds.
**Duration:** 30 s ± 3 s.
**Asset path:** record via Loom; export as MP4; commit to `docs/SolarPro_SendToClient_30s.mp4`; surface via slug `send-to-client-30s`.

---
