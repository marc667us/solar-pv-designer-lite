Common questions
Is the tariff accurate?

Yes. Live PURC Q2 2026 rates, effective 01-04-2026. Twelve customer categories covered. We update each quarter as PURC issues new schedules.

What about levies and VAT?

We compare the energy charge plus service. Levies typically add ~17.5% on top — we show the gap so you can match it to your receipt.

Can I share my result with someone?

Yes — copy link, email me, or invite friends. They open the tool with your numbers prefilled.

Does this require sign-up?

No. The audit + PDF report are free and anonymous. Sign up only if you want the result saved to a SolarPro project so an installer can pick it up.
