Assumptions, limitations & verification
  • All quantities are reproduced from the project's committed PV sizing (Step 7); this report adds no separate sizing engine.
  • Resource (PSH/GHI) and temperatures use the site configuration where provided, else conservative Ghana defaults — confirm against a bankable meteo dataset (e.g. Meteonorm / NASA POWER / SolarGIS) before financial close.
