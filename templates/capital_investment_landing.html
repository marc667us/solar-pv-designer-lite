The 14-step design workflow
1Project Registration — client, investor, developer, jurisdiction, currency, tax regime
2Project Type — ground-mount, rooftop, industrial, utility, IPP, mining, floating, hybrid, BESS
3Site Configuration — land, terrain, slope, soil, flood/wind/seismic, grid distance, ESIA
4Facility Configuration — Control Room, O&M, Battery, Switchgear, Transformer Yard, External Works
5Technology — SCADA, EMS, BMS, CMMS, Digital Twin, Cyber Security
6Electrical System — DC/AC collection, HV/LV distribution, protection
7PV Design — capacity, generation, PR, DC/AC, string, combiner, inverter
8Financial Engineering — CAPEX/OPEX/PPA/NPV/IRR/LCOE/DSCR/Monte Carlo
9BOQ — reuses Section-by-Section + Build-all sections engine
10Marketplace — auto-pulls PV modules, inverters, transformers, RMU, cables
11CRM — opportunity auto-created with capacity, CAPEX, revenue, IRR
12Sales Pipeline — Lead → Concept → Feasibility → Bank → Award → Construction → COD
13Reports — Executive, Technical, Financial, Bankability, Investment Memo, Ops Manual
14AI Agents — PV, Electrical, Civil, Structural, SCADA, Finance, Grid, Reviewer
Plus a Development & Regulatory Configuration sub-step (land tenure, ESIA, grid interconnection, utility approvals, EPA / Energy Commission licensing, building permits, financial close, COD).
