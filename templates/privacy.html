{{ section("p6","6. Data Security") }}

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password hashing using Werkzeug's PBKDF2-SHA256 algorithm
  • CSRF protection on all form submissions
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints
  • Encrypted environment variables for all API keys and secrets

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

{{ section("p7","7. Cookies and Tracking") }}

No tracking cookies: We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies, analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics), or tracking pixels.

Local storage: We use your browser's local storage to remember your display preferences (theme, font size). No personal data is stored in local storage.
