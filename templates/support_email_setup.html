Email / SMTP Setup Guide
Configure SolarPro to send project reports to clients, banks & installers
5 Steps Gmail · Outlook · Brevo · Mailgun ~8 min read
Why Set Up Email?

SolarPro can send project PDF reports directly to clients, banks, and installers. To do this it needs to connect to your outbound mail server (SMTP). Once configured you can:

  • Send any project report from the Email Reports page
  • Receive password reset emails automatically
  • Get system notifications from the platform
1
Open Settings → Email / SMTP
  1. Log in to your SolarPro account.
  2. Click your username in the top-right navigation bar.
  3. Select Settings from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click the Email / SMTP tab at the top of the Settings page.
You will see the SMTP form with fields for host, port, username, password, encryption, and From address.
2
Choose Your Email Provider

Use the Quick-Fill buttons to auto-fill host and port. Supported providers:

3A
Set Up Gmail

Gmail requires an App Password — a 16-character code separate from your Google password.

Generate a Gmail App Password:
  1. Go to myaccount.google.com
  2. Click Security in the left sidebar.
  3. Under "How you sign in to Google", click 2-Step Verification (must be enabled).
  4. Scroll to the bottom → click App passwords.
  5. App: Mail · Device: Other → type "SolarPro" → Generate.
  6. Copy the 16-character password shown.
Enter in Settings → Email / SMTP:
SMTP Hostsmtp.gmail.com
Port587
EncryptionSTARTTLS
Usernameyour.email@gmail.com
Password16-character App Password (NOT your Google password)
From Addressyour.email@gmail.com
Remove spaces when pasting the App Password. Google shows it in groups of 4 — it is one continuous string.
3B
Set Up Brevo Recommended
  1. Sign up free at brevo.com.
  2. Go to Senders & IP → SMTP & API.
  3. Copy your SMTP Login and Master Password (or generate a new SMTP key).
SMTP Hostsmtp-relay.brevo.com
Port587
EncryptionSTARTTLS
UsernameYour Brevo account email
PasswordYour Brevo master password or SMTP key
From AddressA verified sender in your Brevo account
You must verify your From address (or domain) in Brevo → Senders before emails will be delivered.
3C
Set Up Outlook / Microsoft 365
SMTP Hostsmtp.office365.com
Port587
EncryptionSTARTTLS
Usernameyour@company.com
PasswordYour M365 account password
From Addressyour@company.com
4
Test the Connection

After filling in all fields, click Test Connection.

"Connection successful" — SMTP is working. Click Save SMTP.
"Authentication failed" — check your password. Gmail: use the App Password, not your Google password.
"Connection timed out" — check host and port. Port 587 may be blocked by your firewall.
5
Send a Test Report
  1. Open any project with completed calculations.
  2. Click Results → Email Reports.
  3. Enter your own email address in the Recipients field.
  4. Select any report (e.g. BOQ Report).
  5. Click Send Email.
Email arrives within 1–2 minutes. Check your spam folder if not visible in the inbox.
